About me

I'm a Ph.D. student at Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Guofeng Zhang

My ultimate goal is to build compelling Mixed Reality systems with natural user interfaces that people can seamlessly integrate MR into their daily lives. To achieve this, I am advancing localization methods to enhance MR experiences while creating novel, user-centric interactions by combining state-of-the-art technologies in machine learning and sensing. My research in this direction has led to publications to CHI and TVCG. Currently, I am focusing on improving user experiences with Vision-Language Models on smart glasses, exploring the intersection of AI and wearable MR interfaces.
